Our Policies :

  • 1. Use of cell phones : Please do not use the cell phone in patient care areas, consultation rooms, the diagnostic center, the ICUs and OTs. This may disturb critical medical care procedures. Please take care when using cell phones in the room to see that your patient and the other patients are not being disturbed. Please keep cellphone sound to vibration mode at all times when you are in the hospital.

  • 2. Medical Information and Patient Privacy : Krishna Hospital is committed to protecting your medical information and keeping it confidential.

  • 3. Safety : Safety is our concern and we are prepared for any emergency and fire. If you hear an alarm, please stay calm and follow staff instructions. Rest assured your safety is our prime concern.
